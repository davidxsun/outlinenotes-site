# Support — Outline Notes

Need help with **Outline Notes**? The fastest way to reach us is by email: [**themingledspirit@gmail.com**](mailto:themingledspirit@gmail.com).

When you write to us please include:

- Your device model (e.g. iPhone 13 Pro Max, iPad Pro 12.9" M2)
- iOS / iPadOS version
- App version (Settings → Contact → version stamp)
- A short description of the issue and steps to reproduce it

We aim to reply within 2 business days.

## A few common how-tos

### How do I import a printed handout?

Open the **Upload** tab → tap **Camera** (or **Photo Library**) → take/pick one or more photos. Apple Vision on-device OCR converts them to an outline.

### How do I get Bible verses to auto-fetch?

Go to **Settings → Verse Retrieval**. Turn the master toggle ON. For free, public-domain verses, leave the "Rcv" sub-toggle OFF — the bundled World English Bible is used.

For Rcv text, turn the **Rcv** sub-toggle ON, enter your own Rcv API App ID + Token (obtain from <https://api.lsm.org>), and tap **Test**. After a successful test, tap **Save**.

### How do I back up my notes?

- **Automatic**: turn on **Settings → iCloud Sync & Backup**.
- **Manual**: export all notes as a JSON file from the same screen, then re-import on any device.

### How do I delete a note?

Long-press the note in the **Library** → **Delete**. Deleted notes go to Trash for 30 days, then are permanently removed.
